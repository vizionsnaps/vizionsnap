Hi, I'm Tommy!

I am photographer based in Toronto, Canada. I'm also in my third-year of study at Wilfrid Laurier University!

If you know me, you would know that I am a shy and reserved person who absolutely hated being in photos. Luckily, I was able to avoid that problem by being the one who takes the photo. Photography allows me to express my creativity behind the lens. What started out as an escape route turned into a creative outlet for me. It also taught ' me to appreicate the small details in our lives and cherish what we have before it's gone.

Follow along my journey @vizionsnaps on Instagram!
